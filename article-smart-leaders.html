IQ vs Embodiment

Why Smart Leaders Fail at People Skills

The dangerous gap between 'Knowing' and 'Doing'.

We see it constantly: A brilliant engineer or financial wizard gets promoted... and immediately crashes. They have high IQ, but they lack the tools to navigate human dynamics.

The 'Knowing-Doing' Gap

In Neuro-Semantics, we distinguish between Informational Knowledge (Head) and Embodied Competence (Gut). Most leaders try to solve 'People Problems' using 'Logic Tools.'

Employee: "I feel overwhelmed."

Smart Leader: "Here is a logical spreadsheet to fix your schedule."

Result: Disconnect.

The Missing Science

Intelligence is not just processing data; it is processing State. If you cannot calibrate to the emotional energy (Meta-States) of your team, you are flying blind. You don't need to be a therapist, but you do need to master the science of State Management. Without it, your logic is useless.
